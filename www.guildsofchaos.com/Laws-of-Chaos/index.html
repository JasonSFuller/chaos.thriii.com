Guilds of Chaos

Laws of Chaos

These are the Laws of Chaos.  These rules apply to all the guilds that are part of the Chaos family.  Each guild may have different raid rules and schedules based on the current levels of the players in that guild.  They are all based on participation, communication and cooperation and they all follow these fundamental set of principles.  

Earn 600 Tickets

Every member of the Chaos family is expected to earn 600 tickets every day.  Everyone should be able to do this easily.  The guild tracks these contributions closely. If you aren't pulling your weight, you will be notified and booted!

Why is it so important to get 600 tickets every day?

  1. Raids! Raids! Raids! - The more tickets we earn the more often we can raid.  That means more Han Solo and General Kenobi shards and more raid gear which means everyone advances faster and becomes stronger still.
  2. Active members - Let's face it; if you aren't generating 600 tickets per day you're not an active player or your not really trying.  The Chaos family is all about having an active community of people who are participating in the community and in the game.  If that's not you, that's cool, but that's who we are.  We are Chaos!!
  3. It's easy! - If your an active player, you can easily earn 600 tickets per day.  In addition to the continuous refilling of energy that happens over time, you get 180 bonus energy every day (3x45 normal + 45 cantina) and can also buy ​2 normal energy refreshes of 120 for 50 crystals each and 2 cantina energy refreshes of 120 for 100 crystals each.  With this much energy just lying around, there's no reason not to spend 600 EVERY DAY.

​We also look for members to contribute at least 100 energy in daily guild activities that use energy, namely hard mode battles, light and dark side battles and cantina energy.  This is just another indication of your participation.  

​Of course we realize that everyone also has a life outside the game, (well, most of us anyway,) and there will be times when you need to be away from the game.  Maybe a business trip, family emergency, vacation without wifi, etc.  That's fine, but when that happens LET US KNOW.  This brings us to our next Law of Chaos...

Communicate

Communication is the life blood of the Chaos family.  We're not just game players; we're a community. Talk on slack and on in game chat to let us know what your working on and where you could use help.  

Join Slack!  This is a requirement of the Guilds of Chaos.  It is our primary communication channel for the Chaos family and is where we discuss the game, raid plans, character strategies, etc. You can learn more about how to use Slack here.

And finally...

Don't be a Jerk!

We're a community where everyone is here to help each other grow.  We grow as a team, not individuals and we help each other.  Don't be a troll on slack, help your guild mates, give advice and encouragement, etc.  Donate to your team mates when you can. At the same time, don't complain and throw a tantrum if you requested stun cuffs and you don't get a full batch. EVERYONE needs stun cuffs!

Another way to demonstrate that you are a jerk is by breaking raid rules.  Hitting during the 24 hour no damage hold period, going over a damage cap (though we don't have one currently,) etc.  Everyone makes mistakes, of course.  If you communicate that you hit the raid by mistake, we'll get over it.  Repeated offenses will be dealt with, eventually resulting in expulsion from the guild.
