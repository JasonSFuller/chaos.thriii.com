Guilds of Chaos

Guilds

The Guilds of Chaos are a family of guilds for the EA mobile game Star Wars: Galaxy of Heroes.  There are currently 3 guilds, Legacy of Chaos, Legions of Chaos and Society of Chaos.  Most of the guilds run heroic raids for both the pit and AAT.  Some are still developing, but are growing fast!  The whole family is here to help the players in those developing guilds get stronger.  Some of our strongest players join the developing guilds to help them clear raids.  

 

 

Legacy of Chaos

Legacy of Chaos is the original guild of the family of Chaos.  Legacy is a Heroic AAT guild that can clear raids with speed and percision.  You can find our current roster on SWGOH.gg here. 

 

RANCOR HEROIC RAID RULES FOR LEGACY OF CHAOS:

2pm U.S. MST Fixed Raid launch time.

 

300,000 damage CAP for all members. 24 hrs HOLD with 0 dmg.

 

Whoever hits the closest to 300,000 damage (above or below) will have NO RULES on the next Rancor raid and be dubbed Chaos man or lady for that one raid.  Below are the approximate damage precentages to target to hit 300,000 in each phase of the raid:

  • (Pig phase approximately 16.67%)
  • (Phase 2 approximately 10%)
  • (Phase 3 approximately 9%)
  • (Phase 4 approximately 14%)

Learning curve expected, errors excused the first few raids)

 

For ANYONE , 25,000-50,000 damage above cap you will receive a warning and allowed 0 damage on following raid. Guild leaders WILL keep track of names and issue a boot for repeat offense. Beyond 50,000 damage is a boot and reinvite so you dont receive prizes. Repeat offense is permanent boot. 

 

TANK HEROIC RAID RULES FOR LEGACY OF CHAOS:
2pm U.S. MST fixed scheduled launch 
P1-P2 only, P3-P4 opens 24hrs after launch.
Members who achieve General Kenobi 6* or above allowed one squad only per phase.

 

 

Legions of Chaos

Legions of Chaos is the third member of the family of Chaos.  Legions is our second Heroic AAT ready guild  It is currently running a combination of Normal and Heroic AAT raids, sometimes with a little help from Legacy and Fury members to clear them.  While it doesn't yet have the fire power of Fury and Legacy, it is building power quickly and has a great group of members.  You can find the current Legions roster on SWGOH.gg here.

Society of Chaos

Society of Chaos is the latest installment in the Guilds of Chaos and is currently running Heroic Rancor raids.  Society is our developmental guild, but already has some very strong members on board and will grow quickly to join the heroic ranks of the other guilds in the Chaos family.  Following in the footsteps of Legacy and Legions, Society of Chaos will be clearing Heroic Tank Raids in no time!

Society is the guild that new members to the family are typically invited while they prove their mettle in raids and their ability to contribute consistently. Get in on the ground floor and join the Chaos family!
