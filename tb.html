Territory Battles

Fill Platoon Missions first

Contribute as many characters as you can for Platoon Missions, especially if you have rare characters. Do this first, as it helps everyone in the guild. It's better if all players complete more combat rounds, than just you getting higher Combat Mission scores. We all get the same payout; this is a team effort, not a competition.

If you see a platoon that absolutely cannot be completed because of a super rare character (the new Ewoks, 7-star Lobot, Millennium Falcon, etc), do not contribute to it until someone fills those rare slots first.

Complete Combat Missions ASAP

Completing Combat Missions early helps our leaders estimate where to send deployments later on, so knock them out as soon as you possibly can. Remember, start with the most restrictive missions first: Special Missions Rogue One/Phoenix Rebels Light Side.

However, some guilds prioritize Combat Missions over Special Missions in order to maximize stars (for better rewards at the end), but this is the exception, not the rule. Your guild leaders/TB generals will make this clear if this is the case in your guild.

If you are completing Combat Missions late, officers may instruct you to ignore a territory altogether. If so, don't do the Combat Missions until the objectives for the targeted territory have been completed. You can hold a squad in reserve for the extra combats, but if needed, be ready to deploy them to the targeted territory instead. Don't cost the team a star!

Deploy as late as possible

Complete the Deploy Mission as late as possible. If you're in a rush and simply must deploy early, the general rule of thumb is:

  • Deploy to the territory with the fewest stars.
  • Deploy south if both have equal stars.

Use your brain!

Players: When in doubt, look for directions in Slack or the in-game banner. What are the star objectives for this phase? How many people have completed the Combat Missions? Will the remaining mission points be enough to get us to the next star? All of this (and more) factor into the guilds overall strategy. Try to puzzle out the most efficient use of your resources, and as always, ask if you need help. Again, we're a team and rise/fall together.

Officer, generals, guild owners: This means you too! Please be consistent, and don't give contradictory directions. Update the in-game channel banner as the target changes, and reset it as soon as phases are completed, in order to avoid confusion. Or better yet, specify the phase in the banner, for example: "TB-P5: ignore north til we 2* south" ...so when Phase 6 rolls around, players will know these are old orders and no longer apply.
