FAQ

If you have questions, please feel free to ask on the guild chat... or better yet, ask in Slack. We're a big, friendly family. Don't be shy! Others probably have the same questions as you, so don't be surprised if your questions (and answers) show up here.

  • Why is Kung Fu Grip so stupid?

    Because he loves lead paint chips and long walks on the beach with your mom.

  • How do I join Slack?

    First, download the app. Slack has clients for both iOS and Android, plus Windows/Mac/Linux clients for a computer. There's even a web client if you'd rather not install anything, though we highly recommend installing the app on the same device you play SWGOH, since it allows you to recieve notifications if the browser is closed.

    Once you have the app, post your email using the in-game guild chat and ask for a Slack invite.

  • How do I setup a swgoh.gg account?

    First, find your Player ID by going to the home screen and tapping the gear icon. You'll find your Player ID at the bottom of the screen. Take care when copying it, as it is a 20+ character case-sensitive string. Meaning, UPPER and lower are different characters. Don't mix them up! Also, don't share your Player ID with anyone other than swgoh.gg. In some rare cases, it is used for account recovery.

    While you're at it, you might as well get your Ally Code too. From the home screen, tap the Allies button on the left. At the top of the screen you should see your 9 digit Ally Code.

    Now, go create an account at swgoh.gg and log in.

    Finally, go to your settings on swgoh.gg and:

    • Add your 9 digit Ally Code.
    • Check the box to allow other users to see your Ally Code.
    • Check the box to enable collection sync.
    • Enter your 20+ character Player ID.
    • Ensure your profile privacy is set to public.
    • Click the "update profile" button at the bottom.
